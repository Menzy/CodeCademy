About me

I'm Joshua Mensah, but you in paeticular can call me Wan Menzy. I'm a filmmaker, content creator and an aspiring programmer(currently studying Full Stack engineering) and on courase to become a Full Stack developer by December 2023.

I started my filmmaking journey in 2014 while pursuing Communication Design in the Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Ghana. From class projects and personal projects i took on while pursuing the program, i developed great passion for film and knew this was what i wanted to keep doing moving forward. I have since partnered with several brands locally and internationally to create amazing projects on diverse topics. Xiaomi Global, Zhiyun tech, Sandmarc, Moment are but a few of the partnerships i've worked on.

In my bid to keep creating, i became fascinated with building tech tools and challenged myself to learn the skills needed to build apps and websites that would aim to solve some challenges we face in Ghana and Africa as whole. My journey took me to CodeCademy, Programiz Pro and other platforms that I've been engaging with a ton to study the skills to ready me for a programming journey in 2024.

Fun fact! I've been programing for 6,557,895,864 seconds!
