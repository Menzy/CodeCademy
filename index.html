About me

I'm Joshua Mensah, but you can call me Wan Menzy. I'm a versatile creative, encompassing the roles of filmmaker, content creator, and an aspiring programmer. Currently, I'm diligently pursuing my studies in Full Stack Engineering, with the goal of becoming a Full Stack Developer by December 2023.

My filmmaking journey commenced in 2014 during my pursuit of a degree in Communication Design at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Ghana. As I engaged in both class projects and personal endeavors, my passion for film burgeoned, and I realized that this was my true calling. Over time, I've cultivated partnerships with various local and international brands, such as Xiaomi Global, Zhiyun Tech, Sandmarc, and Moment, collaborating on captivating projects spanning a wide range of topics.

Driven by a desire to continue creating, I found myself drawn to the world of technology and felt compelled to acquire the skills necessary for building applications and websites that could address some of the challenges faced in Ghana and across Africa. My educational journey led me to platforms like CodeCademy, Programiz Pro, and others, where I've been diligently honing my programming skills in preparation for a transformative journey into the realm of programming in 2024.
